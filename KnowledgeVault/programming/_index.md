# Programming

**Programming** is the process of designing and building executable software to
accomplish a specific computing result or to perform a particular task.

## Why it matters

Programming underpins everything from websites to spacecraft. A solid grasp of
the fundamentals transfers across every language you'll ever touch.

## Core building blocks

- **Variables & types** — naming and storing data
- **Control flow** — `if`, loops, and branching
- **Functions** — reusable units of behavior
- **Data structures** — arrays, maps, trees, graphs
- **Algorithms** — step-by-step problem solving

> "Programs must be written for people to read, and only incidentally for
> machines to execute." — Harold Abelson
